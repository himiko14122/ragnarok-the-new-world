## Safe Refining Guide - Reaching +15 Without Breaking Equipment

The safe refining system in Ragnarok The New World is one of the most player-friendly mechanics in the game, eliminating the equipment destruction risk that made refinement in the original Ragnarok Online so punishing. Equipment does not break when refinement fails, meaning every Zeny invested in refinement is a permanent improvement rather than a gamble with potential loss. This guide covers the complete safe refining system, success rates at each level, material costs, and strategic approaches for reaching your target refinement efficiently.

Understanding the refinement system is essential because weapon refinement provides the single largest damage increase per investment tier in the game. A weapon refined to +7 deals dramatically more damage than a +0 weapon, and the damage increase continues scaling through +10 and beyond. The safe system removes the emotional and economic trauma of equipment destruction but retains the escalating cost structure that makes high refinement an achievement worth pursuing.

## How Safe Refining Works

### Core Mechanics

The safe refining system allows you to attempt refinement on any equipment piece at a refining NPC. Each attempt costs Zeny and refinement materials, with the cost increasing at each refinement level. Unlike the original Ragnarok Online, a failed refinement attempt does not destroy or downgrade your equipment. Instead, the equipment remains at its current refinement level, and you simply lose the Zeny and materials invested in the attempt.

This no-break mechanic fundamentally changes the economics of refinement. In a break system, reaching high refinement levels requires luck and potentially destroying multiple copies of the same equipment. In the safe system, reaching high refinement requires patience and resources, as every failure is a step backward in wealth but not in equipment progress. The total cost to reach a target refinement level is statistically predictable, allowing players to budget their [Zeny farming](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) accordingly.

### Refinement Level Effects

Each refinement level provides a bonus to the equipment's base stats. For weapons, refinement increases physical and magical attack power. For armor, refinement increases defense. The bonus per level is consistent within each tier, meaning the jump from +6 to +7 provides the same flat bonus as the jump from +3 to +4, though the percentage improvement relative to total stats varies.

## Refinement Strategy

### Weapon First Priority

Always refine your weapon before any other equipment piece. The damage increase from weapon refinement directly accelerates all content, including the [farming](/guides/best-farming-spots) that generates resources for further refinement. A +7 weapon with +0 armor is vastly superior to a +3 weapon with +7 armor because the +7 weapon kills faster, which means less time taking damage and more drops per hour.

### Incremental Target Approach

Set incremental refinement targets rather than attempting to reach your ultimate goal immediately. Start with a realistic target like +4, which is nearly guaranteed. Then push to +7 when you have accumulated sufficient resources. Save for +10 as a long-term goal that requires significant [Zeny investment](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide). This incremental approach provides regular power increases while managing resource risk.

### Kafra VIP Refinement Bonus

The Kafra VIP system provides a refinement success rate bonus that reduces the total cost of reaching higher levels. If you plan to push beyond +10, the VIP refinement bonus significantly reduces the number of failed attempts needed, making it cost-effective for dedicated refinement sessions. Calculate whether the VIP cost is offset by the refinement savings before purchasing.

## FAQ

### Can equipment break in Ragnarok The New World?

No, the safe refining system prevents equipment from breaking or degrading on failed refinement attempts. This is a fundamental difference from the original Ragnarok Online where refinement failure destroyed equipment. In Ragnarok The New World, failed attempts only cost Zeny and materials, never your equipment.

### Is it worth refining past +10?

Refining past +10 provides continued damage increases but at dramatically escalating cost. The value depends on your content goals: for daily farming and dungeon content, +7 to +10 is sufficient. For competitive PvP and the hardest MVP encounters, +12 or higher provides a measurable advantage. The [refinement breakpoints guide](/refining/refinement-breakpoints) covers the specific damage gains at each level.

### Should I refine armor or weapon first?

Always refine your weapon first. Weapon refinement increases damage output, which accelerates all content including resource generation for further investment. Armor refinement improves survivability but does not increase kill speed. The exception is for tank Knights who need to survive specific MVP mechanics, where armor refinement may be necessary for content access.

### How does safe refining compare to the original Ragnarok Online?

The safe system eliminates the most punishing aspect of the original game's refinement system. Players no longer risk losing equipment to failed attempts, making high refinement an investment of time and resources rather than a gamble. This design change makes endgame refinement accessible to [F2P players](/guides/f2p-guide) who can gradually accumulate resources without the risk of catastrophic loss.

### Can I transfer refinement levels between equipment?

Refinement levels are bound to the specific equipment piece and cannot be transferred. When you upgrade to a better equipment piece, the old item's refinement level does not carry over. This makes it important to refine equipment you plan to use long-term rather than spending Zeny on items you will soon replace.
