## Refinement Breakpoints - Damage Gains and Cost Efficiency

Every refinement level in Ragnarok The New World provides a damage increase, but not all levels offer equal value per Zeny invested. Understanding refinement breakpoints helps you identify the most cost-efficient stopping points for your current budget, ensuring you never overspend for marginal gains when the same Zeny could provide larger improvements elsewhere. This guide provides the exact damage contribution at each refinement level, the cost-per-gain ratio, and the recommended breakpoints for different budget tiers.

The concept of breakpoints comes from the observation that refinement value follows a diminishing returns pattern. The first few levels provide enormous damage increases at low cost, while later levels provide smaller relative improvements at exponentially higher cost. Identifying where the cost-per-gain ratio crosses your personal threshold of value helps you make efficient spending decisions.

### Key Breakpoint Analysis

The most significant breakpoints in terms of value are +4, +7, and +10. These represent the points where the cost-per-gain ratio shifts notably.

**+4 Breakpoint**: This is the most efficient refinement target for budget-conscious players. Reaching +4 is nearly guaranteed with minimal material cost, and the +12 physical ATK bonus provides a substantial damage increase that affects all content. For [F2P players](/guides/f2p-guide), +4 should be the first major milestone for any weapon.

**+7 Breakpoint**: The +7 breakpoint represents the sweet spot between cost and benefit for most players. The success rate at 55% means you will occasionally fail, but the cost remains manageable with consistent [Zeny farming](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide). The +21 total ATK bonus provides a meaningful power increase that enables efficient dungeon clearing and MVP contribution.

**+10 Breakpoint**: The +10 breakpoint marks the transition from mid-game to endgame refinement. The 20% success rate means significant failed attempts and resource expenditure, but the +30 ATK bonus separates well-geared characters from the mid-game population. Players targeting competitive PvP or the hardest MVP content should aim for +10 as a long-term goal.

## FAQ

### What refinement level should I stop at?

For most players, +7 is the recommended stopping point for weapons due to its balance of damage gain and cost. Push to +10 only after accumulating significant resources and confirming that your other gear slots are adequately upgraded. The [safe refining guide](/refining/safe-refining-guide) provides detailed cost projections for each target level.

### Does the percentage gain decrease at higher levels?

Yes, the percentage gain relative to your total damage output decreases at higher refinement levels. Going from +0 to +4 might increase your total damage by 15-20%, while going from +10 to +14 might increase it by only 5-8%. However, the absolute damage bonus continues increasing, which matters for content where every point of damage affects clear speed.

### Should I refine multiple weapons or focus on one?

Focus on one primary weapon until it reaches your target level. Refining two weapons to +5 each costs more and provides less total damage benefit than one weapon at +7. The exception is the Gunslinger, who needs two weapons for the swap mechanic, but even then, prioritize one weapon first.

### How do refinement bonuses stack with other damage sources?

Refinement bonuses stack additively with base weapon attack and multiplicatively with percentage-based damage bonuses from cards and buffs. This means the flat ATK bonus from refinement gets amplified by your total percentage damage modifiers, making each point of refinement more valuable as your other bonuses increase.

### Is +15 realistic for F2P players?

Reaching +15 as a F2P player requires exceptional dedication and time investment, potentially a year or more of consistent daily farming. The [Zeny farming guide](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) estimates the income needed to sustain such an investment. Most F2P players should set +10 as a practical long-term maximum and view +12+ as aspirational goals.
