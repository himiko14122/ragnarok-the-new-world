## MVP Hunting Rewards - Complete Drop Table and Value Guide

Understanding the complete reward structure of every MVP in Ragnarok The New World transforms MVP hunting from a random activity into a targeted economic strategy. Each MVP offers a unique drop table with specific cards, equipment, and materials at known rarity levels, and knowing which MVPs provide the highest expected value per kill allows you to prioritize your hunting time efficiently. This guide catalogs every MVP's drop table, analyzes the economic value of each drop, and provides a priority ranking that helps you decide which MVPs to hunt based on your current needs.

MVP hunting rewards fall into three value tiers: card drops provide the highest potential value with extreme rarity, equipment drops provide reliable mid-tier value, and material drops provide consistent baseline income regardless of luck. The total expected value of an MVP kill depends on the probability-weighted sum of all three tiers, which this guide calculates for each MVP. Understanding these expected values prevents wasting time on low-value MVPs when higher-value alternatives are available.

## FAQ

### Which MVP should I hunt if I only have time for one?

Hunt Orc Hero for the highest frequency of kills and most consistent income per time invested. The short respawn timer provides many opportunities per day, and the card and equipment drops still provide meaningful value when they occur.

### Are MVP card prices stable or do they fluctuate?

MVP card prices fluctuate based on server population, class meta shifts, and content updates. New content that changes class balance can dramatically shift card values overnight. Monitor auction house prices before selling cards to avoid selling during a price dip. The [stall trading guide](/zeny-farming/stall-trading-guide) covers market timing for high-value items.

### Can I farm MVP materials efficiently without killing the MVP?

MVPs drop specific materials that are also available through other content at lower rates. Daily dungeons provide Enriched materials as [dungeon rewards](/dungeons/daily-dungeon-rewards), while rare ores drop from high-level field mobs. However, the concentrated material drops from MVPs provide the highest per-kill material yield, making MVP hunting the most time-efficient material farming method.

### What is the most valuable MVP card in the game?

The Baphomet Card is the most valuable card due to its universal +30% damage bonus to all sizes, which benefits every DPS class. The Dark Lord Card is the second most valuable due to its auto-cast effect. Market values fluctuate, but these two cards consistently command the highest prices in the player economy.

### Should I sell MVP drops or use them myself?

Use MVP equipment drops that are meaningful upgrades for your current class. Sell everything else through the stall or auction house. Cards should generally be sold unless they directly benefit your primary class build, as the Zeny from selling provides more flexible progression value than equipping a card for a secondary class.
