## MVP Mount List - All Boss Mounts and Where to Find Them

MVP mounts represent the pinnacle of mount collection in Ragnarok The New World, combining distinctive visual designs with superior movement stats that set them apart from every other mount in the game. Each MVP that drops a mount item provides a unique creature with its own aesthetic theme, movement characteristics, and prestige value. This guide catalogs every MVP mount available in the game, detailing which bosses drop them, the estimated drop rates, and the specific stats and abilities each mount provides.

Collecting MVP mounts is a long-term goal that spans the entire endgame experience. The extremely low drop rates mean most MVP mounts are obtained after dozens or hundreds of boss kills, requiring dedication to consistent [MVP hunting](/mvp-hunting/mvp-hunting-guide) over weeks and months. This guide helps you prioritize which MVP mounts to pursue by comparing their stats, visual appeal, and acquisition difficulty, allowing you to focus your hunting efforts on the mounts that matter most to your collection goals.

The mount features a thick armored hide and tusks that give it a distinctive silhouette compared to other ground mounts. The Orc Mount is one of the more accessible MVP mounts due to Orc Hero's relatively common spawn rate.

## Acquisition Strategy

### Targeting Specific Mounts

Rather than hunting random MVPs, focus on the specific MVP that drops your desired mount. Track the [spawn timers](/mvp-hunting/mvp-spawn-timers) for your target MVP and participate in every available kill. Consistent participation accumulates attempts faster than occasional random hunting, significantly improving your odds over time.

### Economic Alternative

If you have substantial [Zeny savings](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide), purchasing mount items from the auction house provides a guaranteed acquisition path. Ground mount items typically cost 2-10M Zeny, while flying mount items cost 20-100M+ Zeny depending on the server economy. The [stall trading guide](/zeny-farming/stall-trading-guide) covers income strategies for accumulating this level of wealth.

### Guild Coordination

Active guilds that organize [MVP hunting parties](/mvp-hunting/mvp-hunting-guide) provide the most efficient path to MVP mount collection. Guild groups secure more MVP kills per week than solo hunters, increasing the total number of drop opportunities. Discuss mount distribution within your guild to avoid internal competition for mount drops.

## FAQ

### Which MVP mount should I pursue first?

Target the Orc Mount from Orc Hero as your first MVP mount. Orc Hero has the shortest respawn timer and the highest drop rate among MVP mounts, making it the most accessible target. The Orc Mount also provides a meaningful movement speed bonus that improves your daily efficiency while you pursue rarer mounts.

### Are MVP mount items tradeable?

Most MVP mount items are tradeable before they are used. Once used to tame the mount, it becomes permanently bound to your account. This tradeability allows you to purchase mount items from other players through the auction house, providing an alternative acquisition method for players with sufficient [Zeny](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide).

### Can I use MVP mounts on all my classes?

Yes, all mounts are account-wide and available to every class through the [Job Freedom system](/guides/job-freedom-guide). When you switch classes, your mount collection remains accessible. This universal availability makes investing in an MVP mount extremely efficient, as the benefit applies across your entire class portfolio.

### Is there a difference in flight mechanics between MVP flying mounts?

All flying mounts share the same core flight mechanics, including altitude control, stamina system, and dismount behavior. The differences between MVP flying mounts are purely statistical, with the Demon Wing providing the highest flight speed and the Storm Eagle offering the best combination of speed and accessibility.

### How do MVP mounts compare to tamed flying mounts?

MVP flying mounts provide higher flight speed and stamina than any non-MVP flying mount. The free Basic Eagle from the [wing unlock quest](/mounts/free-wing-unlock-quest) provides +40% flight speed, while the Storm Eagle provides +70%. This 30% difference significantly impacts travel time across the continent. However, the free Basic Eagle is sufficient for accessing all sky-only content.
