## Free Wing Unlock Quest - Your First Flying Mount

Unlocking free flight in Ragnarok The New World is a defining moment in every player's journey, transforming how you navigate the continent and opening access to exclusive sky-only content. The Free Wing Unlock questline provides a permanent flying mount at no cost, making it one of the most important quest chains to complete as early as possible. This guide provides a detailed step-by-step walkthrough of the entire questline, including prerequisite requirements, each quest objective, optimal routes between objectives, and tips for completing the quest efficiently regardless of your class or equipment level.

The Free Wing Unlock quest is designed to be completable by any class at the appropriate level, requiring no special equipment or party composition. The questline teaches flight mechanics progressively, starting with ground mount tutorials and building toward full aerial navigation by the final step. Completing this quest at the earliest available level dramatically improves your daily efficiency through faster travel, making it a priority milestone that should not be delayed.

## Prerequisites

### Level Requirements

The Free Wing Unlock quest becomes available at base level 45. This level gate ensures players have experienced enough of the game world to understand the geography before gaining the ability to fly across it. If you have not reached level 45 yet, follow the [leveling guide](/guides/leveling-guide) for the fastest path to eligibility.

### Quest Prerequisites

Before the Free Wing Unlock quest appears, you must complete:

1. The main story questline through Chapter 8
2. The "Winds of Change" side quest in Izlude
3. The mount taming tutorial from the Prontera stable master

These prerequisite quests introduce the mount system and provide narrative context for the flight unlock. The [beginner guide](/guides/beginner-guide) covers the early story progression needed to reach these prerequisites.

### Recommended Preparation

While the quest is completable at any equipment level, having a ground mount significantly speeds up travel between quest objectives. Complete the [mount taming guide](/mounts/mount-taming-guide) to obtain a basic ground mount before starting this questline for the most efficient experience.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Step 1: Accept the Quest at Kafra HQ

**Location**: Izlude, Kafra Headquarters
**NPC**: Kafra Flight Instructor
**Objective**: Speak to the Kafra Flight Instructor to begin the "Wings of Freedom" quest

The Kafra Flight Instructor stands near the Kafra service point in Izlude. She introduces the concept of flight and directs you to collect three Wind Crystals from the eastern cliffs. This introductory dialogue establishes the narrative framing for the questline and provides your first flight-related item, the Wind Compass, which highlights quest objectives on your map.

### Step 2: Collect Wind Crystals from Izlude Cliffs

**Location**: Izlude East Cliffs
**Objective**: Collect 3 Wind Crystals from sparkling nodes along the cliff path
**Time**: 10-15 minutes

The Wind Crystals spawn at fixed locations along the eastern cliff path in Izlude. The crystals appear as glowing blue nodes that you interact with to collect. The path is linear and safe from aggressive monsters, making this step straightforward. Collecting all three crystals completes the second quest step and unlocks the next objective.

**Tip**: Use your ground mount to travel between crystal nodes faster. The nodes are spaced approximately 30 seconds apart on foot, but a mount reduces this to under 15 seconds per node.

### Step 3: Deliver Crystals to the Wind Sage

**Location**: Prontera Wind Tower
**NPC**: Wind Sage Aldric
**Objective**: Deliver Wind Crystals and receive the Sky Feather item

The Wind Sage is located at the Wind Tower in northern Prontera. Delivering the three Wind Crystals triggers a dialogue where the Sage explains the history of flight in Midgard and gives you the Sky Feather, a key item needed for the upcoming taming attempt. The Wind Sage also teaches you the basic flight theory that governs the game's aerial mechanics.

### Step 4: Tame the Wind Eagle at the Mountain Pass

**Location**: Mountain Pass east of Prontera
**Objective**: Use the Sky Feather to tame the Wind Eagle
**Difficulty**: Moderate (taming minigame)

This is the first challenging step of the questline. The Wind Eagle spawns at a specific point in the Mountain Pass and requires the Sky Feather taming item. Approach the Wind Eagle and use the Sky Feather to initiate the taming minigame. The minigame involves a moderate-difficulty quick-time event with approximately 8 input sequences.

**Taming Tips**: Focus on the center of the screen where the input prompts appear. Avoid distractions from the visual effects during the minigame. If you fail the first attempt, the Wind Eagle respawns after 5 minutes, allowing you to try again with a new Sky Feather from the Wind Sage.

### Step 5: Complete the First Flight Trial

**Location**: Sky Tower entrance
**Objective**: Fly through 3 checkpoint rings in sequence
**Time**: 5-10 minutes

After taming the Wind Eagle, you must demonstrate basic flight control by flying through three checkpoint rings suspended in the air near the Sky Tower. The rings test altitude control, turning, and speed management. Fly through each ring in order — the next ring only activates after you pass through the previous one.

**Flight Tips**: Maintain a steady forward speed while approaching each ring. Do not accelerate too fast, as overshooting the ring forces you to circle back. The rings are spaced to be reachable at moderate flight speed, not maximum speed. Practice altitude control by flying level between rings rather than climbing and diving.

### Step 6: Navigate the Aerial Course

**Location**: Open sky above Midgard
**Objective**: Complete the full aerial navigation course with 8 checkpoints
**Time**: 10-15 minutes

The aerial navigation course extends the checkpoint ring concept across a longer route that spans multiple zones. This step tests sustained flight, stamina management, and directional navigation. The course includes tight turns, altitude changes, and a stamina-draining long-distance segment that requires efficient flight pathing.

**Stamina Management**: Conserve stamina during the early straight segments by maintaining moderate speed rather than maximum speed. The long-distance segment near the end requires full stamina, so arrive at this segment with at least 70% stamina remaining. Glide briefly between checkpoints to regenerate stamina if needed.

### Step 7: Land at the Sky Sanctuary and Claim Your Wings

**Location**: Sky Sanctuary (elevated platform)
**Objective**: Land precisely on the Sky Sanctuary platform and speak to the Wing Master
**Reward**: Permanent Basic Eagle flying mount

The final step requires a precise landing on the Sky Sanctuary platform. Approach the platform from above at moderate speed, reduce altitude gradually, and land within the designated zone. Once landed, speak to the Wing Master NPC who congratulates you and permanently adds the Basic Eagle to your mount collection.

## FAQ

### Can I fail the quest and lose progress?

Individual quest steps can be retried if failed. The taming minigame for the Wind Eagle allows unlimited attempts with replacement Sky Feathers from the Wind Sage. The flight trials can be restarted from the beginning if you miss checkpoints. There is no permanent failure state in the questline.

### What level should I be to complete this quest comfortably?

The quest becomes available at level 45, which is the recommended level for completion. There are no combat encounters in the questline, so equipment level does not matter. The only challenges are the taming minigame and the flight navigation, both of which depend on player skill rather than character stats.

### Can I complete the quest on any class?

Yes, the Free Wing Unlock quest is class-agnostic and does not require specific skills or abilities. Any class from the [Job Freedom system](/guides/job-freedom-guide) can complete the quest. The mount reward is account-wide, so you can complete the quest on any class and use the flying mount on all classes.

### Is the Basic Eagle mount good enough or should I upgrade?

The Basic Eagle provides adequate flight capability for all content in the game. Upgrading to a faster flying mount through the [taming system](/mounts/mount-taming-guide) or [MVP drops](/mounts/mvp-mount-list) improves travel speed and stamina but does not unlock additional content. The Basic Eagle is functionally sufficient; upgrades are for convenience and prestige.

### How long does the full questline take to complete?

The complete questline takes approximately 45-60 minutes for an experienced player. New players unfamiliar with flight controls may take 60-90 minutes, particularly on the aerial navigation course. The quest is designed as a single-session activity that does not require multiple days to complete.
