## Beginner Guide for Ragnarok The New World

Welcome to Ragnarok The New World, the modern reimagining of the beloved Ragnarok Online franchise set in the vast, seamless open world of Midgard. Whether you are a veteran of the original game or completely new to the franchise, this guide covers everything you need to know during your first hours and days in the game. From character creation and class selection to understanding core systems like the Job Freedom mechanic, this beginner guide provides a clear path through the early game that avoids common pitfalls and sets you up for efficient long-term progression.

Ragnarok The New World distinguishes itself from other mobile MMORPGs through several key features: the Job Freedom system that allows class switching without creating new characters, a safe refinement system that prevents equipment destruction, an open world with real-time lighting and weather, and a player-driven economy through stall trading and auction houses. Understanding these systems early prevents wasted resources and accelerates your progression toward the more rewarding mid-game and endgame content.

## Character Creation and First Steps

### Choosing Your First Class

Your first class choice matters for early progression, but thanks to Job Freedom, it is not permanent. The eight available classes are Swordsman, Mage, Archer, Acolyte, Thief, Merchant, Gunslinger, and Druid. For beginners, the [Swordsman](/classes/swordsman-knight-build) is recommended due to its natural survivability and straightforward mechanics. The Mage offers the fastest leveling through AoE spells, while the Archer provides a safe ranged experience.

Avoid the Merchant and Druid as your first class. The Merchant's weak combat makes early leveling tedious, and the Druid's three-form system overwhelms new players with complexity. You can switch to these classes later using Job Freedom once you have established a stable economic and equipment foundation.

### Tutorial Completion

The tutorial in Ragnarok The New World guides you through basic movement, combat, and the first questline in Izlude. Complete the entire tutorial before exploring, as it unlocks essential features like the inventory system, skill bar configuration, and the minimap. The tutorial also provides your first set of equipment and introduces the Kafra service system that handles storage and teleportation.

### Redeem Codes Immediately

Before spending significant time on gameplay, redeem all available gift codes for free resources. Check our [all redeem codes page](/codes/all-redeem-codes) for the current list. Code rewards provide Bound Zeny, Adventure Coins, and Kafra Blind Box tickets that significantly accelerate your early progression. Enter codes from the login screen or the in-game settings menu.

## Core Systems to Understand

### Job Freedom System

The Job Freedom system is the most important mechanic to understand as a beginner. Unlike traditional class systems where your choice is permanent, Ragnarok The New World allows you to switch between all eight classes freely. Your base level carries over between classes, but your job level resets to level 1 for the new class. Equipment specific to your previous class is retained in storage but cannot be equipped by the new class.

This system means you should not stress about your initial class selection. Play what feels comfortable, learn the game mechanics, and switch when you want to try something different. Many experienced players maintain two to three classes, using a combat class for progression content and an economic class like the Merchant for [income generation](/zeny-farming/merchant-economy-guide).

### Safe Refinement System

The refinement system in Ragnarok The New World is significantly more forgiving than the original Ragnarok Online. Equipment does not break when refinement fails, making the upgrade process less punishing. Safe refinement allows equipment to reach +15 through a gradual progression of increasing cost and success rates. Always prioritize weapon refinement first, as it provides the largest damage increase per Zeny invested.

### Economy Basics

The in-game economy revolves around Zeny, the primary tradeable currency, and Bound Zeny, which cannot be traded between players. Adventure Coins serve as a premium currency for convenience items. Understanding the distinction between these currencies helps you allocate resources correctly. Zeny from combat drops and [farming activities](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) is tradeable and can be used in the auction house and stall trading systems. Bound Zeny from codes and quest rewards is restricted to NPC transactions.

## Early Progression Path

### Level 1-30: Foundation Phase

During the foundation phase, focus on completing the main story quests which provide experience, equipment, and unlock new areas. Follow the quest markers from Izlude through the surrounding fields, gradually expanding your map knowledge. Do not skip dialogue entirely, as some quests provide important system unlocks and Kafra service access points.

### Level 30-60: Growth Phase

The growth phase introduces second job advancement, which is the first major power spike in your progression. Reach job level 40 to qualify for the second job quest, which involves a series of trials specific to your class. The second job advancement unlocks your defining skills: Bowling Bash for Knights, Meteor Storm for Wizards, ADL for Hunters, and so on. These skills fundamentally change your gameplay and dramatically increase your effectiveness.

### Level 60+: Content Phase

Once you reach level 60 and complete second job advancement, the game opens up significantly. [MVP hunting](/mvp-hunting/mvp-hunting-guide), dungeon content like the [Time Corridor](/dungeons/time-corridor-guide), and PvP activities become your primary progression paths. Your daily routine at this stage should include daily dungeons, MVP participation, and [Zeny farming](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) for equipment refinement.

## Common Beginner Mistakes

### Spreading Resources Too Thin

New players often try to refine all equipment equally, spending Zeny across multiple items instead of concentrating on their weapon. Weapon refinement provides the highest damage return per investment tier. A +7 weapon with +0 armor outperforms a +3 weapon with +3 armor in virtually every scenario.

### Ignoring the Stall Trading System

The stall trading system allows you to sell items to other players while offline. Many beginners vendor valuable materials to NPCs for a fraction of their market value. Before selling any material, check the stall trading prices to see if another player will pay significantly more. This single habit can double or triple your early Zeny income.

### Skipping Daily Activities

Daily dungeons and events provide guaranteed rewards that compound over time. Skipping even a few days of daily content means missing resources that are difficult to recover later. Establish a [daily routine](/guides/daily-routine) early that includes all available daily activities.

### Neglecting Skill Point Planning

While skill reset options exist, wasting skill points on suboptimal skills delays your progression. Research your class build before allocating points, focusing on core skills that define your class role. For build recommendations, see our [class builds section](/classes/all-classes-builds).

## FAQ

### How long does it take to reach second job advancement?

Most players reach job level 40 for second job advancement within two to three days of moderate play. Using experience boosters from Adventure Coins and following the main quest line accelerates this process. The [leveling guide](/guides/leveling-guide) provides optimized grinding paths for each level range.

### Can I play Ragnarok The New World on both PC and mobile?

Yes, Ragnarok The New World supports cross-platform play between PC through Steam and mobile through Google Play and the App Store. Your account progress syncs across platforms, allowing seamless switching between devices. The PC version supports 120 FPS for smoother gameplay.

### What is the Job Freedom system?

Job Freedom allows you to switch between all eight classes on a single character without creating a new character. Your base level carries over, but job level resets for the new class. This system reduces the risk of class selection and lets you experience all playstyles. See our dedicated [Job Freedom guide](/guides/job-freedom-guide) for details.

### How do I get a mount?

Mounts are obtained through the taming system, which involves encountering wild mounts in the open world and successfully taming them. Some MVP bosses drop rare mount items. Flying mounts unlock at higher levels and require specific taming conditions. Our [mount taming guide](/mounts/mount-taming-guide) covers the complete process.

### Is Ragnarok The New World pay-to-win?

The game includes cash shop items and gacha mechanics but is considered reasonably F2P-friendly compared to similar titles. The safe refinement system prevents equipment loss, and most content is achievable through time investment rather than spending. See our [F2P guide](/guides/f2p-guide) for a detailed analysis of progression without spending money. The official Steam page provides more details about the game at [store.steampowered.com](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2689390/Ragnarok_The_New_World/).
