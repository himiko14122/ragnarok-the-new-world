## How to Get Free Wings in Ragnarok The New World

Wings are one of the most visually distinctive cosmetic items in Ragnarok The New World, and obtaining them for free is a common goal for both new and veteran players. Wings serve as a back accessory that provides a dramatic visual effect while your character moves through Midgard. While some wings are available through the cash shop or gacha system, several wing variants can be unlocked entirely through free gameplay methods. This guide covers every known method for obtaining free wings, from quest rewards and achievement unlocks to event participation and code rewards.

The wing system in Ragnarok The New World is purely cosmetic and does not provide combat statistics. This means every player, regardless of spending level, can enjoy the visual appeal of wings without feeling compelled to purchase them for gameplay advantages. The cosmetic nature of wings aligns with the game's design philosophy of keeping combat power separate from visual customization.

## Free Wing Sources

### Main Story Quest Rewards

The primary method for obtaining free wings is through the main story questline. As you progress through the narrative from Izlude to Prontera and beyond, specific story milestones award cosmetic items including a basic wing set. The story-awarded wings are typically a simple design compared to premium variants, but they provide the core visual effect that players associate with the wing accessory.

Complete the main story quests through Chapter 5 to receive the first free wing reward. The questline naturally guides you through this progression, so simply following the story ensures you receive the wings. There is no specific side quest or hidden requirement; the wings arrive as part of the standard story reward sequence.

### Achievement System Wings

The achievement system provides additional wing unlocks for completing specific milestones. These achievement wings are more visually elaborate than the story quest wings and demonstrate dedication to specific gameplay activities.

| Achievement Category | Requirement | Wing Reward |
|---------------------|-------------|-------------|
| Exploration | Visit all major cities | Traveler Wings |
| Combat | Defeat 100 MVPs | MVP Hunter Wings |
| Economy | Earn 10 million total Zeny | Merchant Wings |
| Social | Join a guild and reach rank 3 | Guild Wings |

Each achievement wing requires sustained effort in its respective category. The MVP Hunter Wings, for example, require participating in [MVP hunting](/mvp-hunting/mvp-hunting-guide) consistently over time. The Merchant Wings demand significant [economic activity](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) through trading and vending.

### Event Participation Wings

Seasonal events and special celebrations frequently offer limited-time wing variants as participation rewards. These event wings are exclusive to their respective events and cannot be obtained after the event concludes, making them collectible items that demonstrate participation in specific time periods.

Recent events that provided free wings include the SEA launch celebration, which awarded commemorative wings to all players who logged in during the first week, and the Bangkok carnival event that distributed festival-themed wings through a minigame participation system. Check the event calendar regularly and participate in events offering wing rewards to expand your collection.

## Flying Mounts vs Wings

It is important to distinguish between cosmetic wings and flying mounts in Ragnarok The New World. Cosmetic wings are back accessories that provide a visual effect but do not enable flight. Flying mounts are separate creatures that allow aerial travel across the open world. While wings look similar to mount wings, they serve different functions in the game.

Flying mounts unlock through the taming system at higher levels and require specific conditions to obtain. The [flying mounts guide](/mounts/flying-mounts-guide) covers the taming process and mount types in detail. Cosmetic wings, by contrast, are available immediately upon earning the reward and provide their visual effect on the ground and in the air.

## Wing Customization

### Dyeing System

The dyeing system in Ragnarok The New World extends to certain wing types, allowing color customization beyond the default appearance. Hair dye and fashion dye items from [redeem codes](/codes/code-rewards-list) sometimes include wing dye variants that change wing color. The dyeing system provides additional visual diversity without purchasing multiple wing variants.

### Wing Animation Types

Different wing types feature distinct animation patterns. Story quest wings typically have a gentle flutter animation, while achievement wings feature more dynamic movement patterns. Event wings sometimes include special particle effects that create unique visual signatures. The animation variety means each wing type provides a distinct visual identity beyond simple color differences.

## Tips for Free Wing Collectors

### Prioritize Story Quest Wings First

The story quest wings require the least effort and should be your first acquisition. Follow the [beginner guide](/guides/beginner-guide) through the main story to reach the Chapter 5 milestone that awards these wings. This also advances your overall progression, making it the most efficient use of your time.

### Track Achievement Progress

Monitor your achievement progress toward wing unlocks in the achievement interface. Focus on categories where you are closest to the wing threshold. If you have already visited most cities, for example, completing the remaining city visits for the Traveler Wings requires minimal additional effort.

### Never Miss Event Wings

Event wings are the most time-sensitive because they become unobtainable after the event concludes. When an event offers wings as a reward, prioritize participation even if the event is not otherwise interesting to you. The permanent collection value of exclusive wings exceeds the temporary value of other event rewards.

## FAQ

### Do wings provide any combat bonuses?

No, wings in Ragnarok The New World are purely cosmetic items that do not provide combat statistics, attribute bonuses, or gameplay advantages. They exist exclusively for visual customization and character appearance personalization.

### Can I wear wings while mounted?

Yes, cosmetic wings remain visible while riding ground mounts. When using a flying mount, the mount's natural wings take visual precedence, but your cosmetic wings may still appear depending on the specific mount and wing combination.

### How many free wing types are available?

Currently, there are approximately four to six free wing types available through story quests, achievements, and events. The exact number changes as new events introduce limited-time wings and updates add new achievements. Premium wing variants through the cash shop provide additional options beyond the free selection.

### Can I trade wings to other players?

Free wing rewards from quests and achievements are typically bound to your account and cannot be traded. Event wings follow the same binding restriction. Only certain cash shop wing items may be tradeable, depending on their specific item classification.

### Will more free wings be added?

The development team regularly adds new cosmetic items through content updates and events. Expect additional free wing options through future achievements, story expansions, and seasonal events. Following the official Discord and community channels provides advance notice of upcoming wing reward opportunities.
