## Offline Auto-Hunting

### How Auto-Hunting Works

Offline auto-hunting allows your character to continue farming mobs while you are logged out, generating passive Zeny income during your offline hours. The auto-hunting system places your character in a designated hunting zone where it automatically attacks nearby mobs and collects drops. The income rate is significantly lower than active farming but accumulates during hours when you cannot actively play.

### Auto-Hunting Setup

1. Visit the Kafra NPC and select the auto-hunting option
2. Choose your hunting zone from available options based on your level
3. Equip your combat gear and ensure sufficient arrow/skill supplies
4. Activate auto-hunting and log out

The system uses your character's actual combat stats, making [equipment investment](/refining/safe-refining-guide) directly impactful on auto-hunting income. Better equipment means faster kills and more drops during auto-hunting sessions.

### Auto-Hunting Income by Zone

| Zone | Level Range | Zeny/Hour | 8-Hour Income | Requirements |
|------|------------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Einbroch Mines | 50-70 | 25K-40K | 200K-320K | Level  Bow or Staff |
| Clock Tower | 60-80 | 30K-50K | 240K-400K | Level  AoE capability |
| Niflheim Edge | 70-90 | 35K-55K | 280K-440K | Level  Weapon, elemental prep |
| Abyss Lake Outskirts | 90+ | 40K-65K | 320K-520K | Level  Weapon, high ASPD |

Auto-hunting income represents approximately 30-40% of total F2P daily income, making it the most important passive income source. Always set auto-hunting before logging out to avoid missing this income. The auto-hunting activation uses one daily auto-hunting charge that resets daily.

### Auto-Hunting Limitations

- Only one auto-hunting session per day (resets at daily reset)
- Maximum duration of 12 hours per session
- Income is approximately 30% of active farming rate at the same location
- Your character cannot participate in other activities during auto-hunting
- If your equipment is insufficient for the zone, auto-hunting fails and generates minimal income

## F2P Market Strategies

### Buy-Low, Sell-High Without Capital

F2P players with limited starting capital can still profit from market trading by focusing on high-turnover, low-margin items. SP Potions and buff foods have consistent demand and small price fluctuations that create trading opportunities. Buy during off-peak hours when supply exceeds demand and sell during peak hours when demand surges.

### Event Material Flipping

During seasonal events, event-specific materials temporarily flood the market at low prices. Purchase these materials at event-start prices and hold them until the event ends, when supply drops and prices rise. This strategy requires patience but generates significant returns for minimal effort. The [stall trading guide](/zeny-farming/stall-trading-guide) covers event-specific market patterns.

### Code Item Monetization

Redeem code items that provide tradeable rewards generate free inventory for stall selling. Some code rewards include materials and consumables that sell for 10K-50K Zeny each. Always redeem available [codes](/codes/all-redeem-codes) immediately and assess which rewards are worth selling versus using.

## FAQ

### Is it really possible to earn 1.5M Zeny daily as F2P?

Yes, with a consistent daily routine that includes dungeon runs, active farming, stall trading, and overnight auto-hunting, approximately 1.5M daily is achievable. Individual days vary based on drop luck, but the monthly average for dedicated F2P players consistently reaches this level. The [Zeny farming guide](/zeny-farming/zeny-farming-guide) provides the complete income breakdown.

### How does auto-hunting compare to active farming?

Auto-hunting generates approximately 30% of the hourly income you would earn actively farming the same location. The advantage is that it operates during hours you cannot play, adding 200K-500K daily income that requires no active time investment. Always activate auto-hunting before logging out.

### Can F2P players compete in PvP?

Yes, PvP arena normalization reduces equipment disparities, allowing skilled F2P players to compete effectively. War of Emperium does not normalize, giving spending players an advantage, but F2P players contribute meaningfully through coordinated play. The [best PvP classes guide](/pvp/best-pvp-classes) covers class selection for F2P PvP viability.

### What should my first major Zeny purchase be?

A +4 weapon for your primary class. This single investment dramatically improves farming efficiency, which accelerates all future Zeny generation. After +4, save toward +7 for Hard mode dungeon access and higher-tier farming spots. The [safe refining guide](/refining/safe-refining-guide) covers the refinement process.

### How long does it take an F2P player to reach endgame equipment?

F2P players typically reach endgame equipment levels (weapon +10, armor +7) within 4-6 months of consistent daily play. Spending players reach the same level in 2-3 months.
