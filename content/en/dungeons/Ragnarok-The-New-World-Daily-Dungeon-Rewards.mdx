## Daily Dungeon Rewards and Reset Guide

Daily dungeons form the core progression loop in Ragnarok The New World, providing guaranteed refinement materials, equipment drops, and Adventure Coins that fuel every aspect of character advancement. Understanding the complete reward structure of each dungeon, the daily reset schedule, and how to prioritize your limited entries determines whether you progress steadily or fall behind the server curve. This guide compiles every dungeon's reward table, explains the reset system in detail, and provides a daily priority framework that maximizes your resource income regardless of your available playtime.

The dungeon system operates on a daily entry ticket model where each player receives a fixed number of entries per dungeon per day. Entries reset at the server's daily reset time, and unused entries are lost permanently. This system creates a daily obligation to spend your entries efficiently, as missing a day means missing rewards that cannot be recovered later. The key to efficient dungeon farming is knowing which dungeons to prioritize, which difficulty modes offer the best return on time investment, and how to sequence your dungeon runs within your daily schedule.

## Reset Schedule

### Daily Reset Mechanics

All dungeon entries reset simultaneously at the server's daily reset time. The reset refreshes all dungeon entry counts to their maximum, regardless of how many entries were used the previous day. Partially completed runs at the time of reset are invalidated without reward, so plan your daily schedule to finish all in-progress runs before reset.

### Weekly Reset Additions

In addition to the daily reset, a weekly reset occurs that refreshes weekly dungeon bonuses and adds extra entry tickets for specific dungeons. The weekly reset typically includes bonus Adventure Coin rewards for the first completion of each dungeon during the week. Plan your weekly schedule to complete at least one run of each dungeon during the weekly bonus window for maximum value.

### Reset Timing Table

| Reset Type | Frequency | Effect | Carry-Over |
|------------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Daily Reset | Every 24 hours | Entry tickets refreshed | None |
| Weekly Reset | Every 7 days | Bonus entries, weekly rewards | None |
| Event Reset | Varies | Bonus tickets, special rewards | Varies |

## FAQ

### What time does the daily dungeon reset occur?

The daily dungeon reset occurs at the server's scheduled daily reset time, which is displayed in the dungeon interface. All dungeon entries refresh simultaneously at this time. Plan to complete your dungeon runs at least 15 minutes before reset to avoid last-minute timing issues.

### Can I save unused dungeon entries for the next day?

No, unused dungeon entries are permanently lost at the daily reset. There is no carry-over mechanism or banking system for entries. This design encourages daily engagement and prevents resource accumulation through absence. Make completing your daily dungeon runs a consistent habit to avoid wasted entries.

### Should I run Normal or Hard mode with limited entries?

Always run Hard mode if your party can clear it consistently, as the reward value per entry is approximately double Normal mode. If Hard mode is unreliable, mix Normal and Hard entries to ensure you use all entries rather than wiping repeatedly on Hard mode and losing entries to failed attempts. See the [Time Corridor guide](/dungeons/time-corridor-guide) for difficulty comparison details.

### How do I get additional dungeon entries beyond the daily count?

Additional entries are available through premium items from the cash shop, event bonuses, and occasionally through the Kafra Blind Box system. F2P players should monitor events that provide bonus entries, as these represent free progression opportunities. The [redeem codes guide](/codes/all-redeem-codes) sometimes includes items that grant extra dungeon entries.

### Are dungeon rewards affected by my character's level?

Dungeon rewards are fixed per dungeon and difficulty mode, regardless of character level. However, higher-level characters clear faster, improving the time efficiency of each entry. There is no penalty for running dungeons at higher levels, making daily dungeon farming valuable content at every stage of progression.

### Can I share dungeon entries between characters?

Dungeon entries are character-specific and cannot be shared between characters on the same account. Each character has its own daily entry pool. If you have multiple characters at dungeon-eligible levels, each provides independent income through daily dungeon runs.
